About

WSUCon is an 24 hour annual social and gaming event that brings together WSU students, faculty, and the public in a fun and lively environment. Now in its 18th iteration, you can expect the largest WSUCon yet. WSUCon is free to attend because of the generous donations from our sponsors.


We are planning on the biggest WSUCon yet, complete with even more gaming space, more bandwidth, and more unquestionable gaming glory. Check out the schedule and FAQs for more info. If you want to see what the con has looked like in the past, check out the pictures from previous years.


Come meet new people and have a blast! We can't wait to see you there!
