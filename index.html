About

WSUCon is an annual social and gaming event, put on by the Linux Users' Group, that brings WSU students and faculty together in a fun and lively environment. Now in its 15th iteration, you can expect the largest WSUCon yet. WSUCon is free to attend, through the donations of our community and sponsors (see sponsors page), and we always hope to keep it so! This year, we will be switching up how many things are done, so please pay attention as we don't want to turn anyone away.


We are planning on the biggest WSUCon yet, complete with more space, more bandwidth, and more unquestionable gaming glory. Check out the Tournaments and FAQ for more info. If you want to see what the con has looked like in the past, check out these Pictures from previous years.


Tespa and Twitch are sponsoring prizes for this event! Attendants can win these awesome prizes by entering official WSUCON tournaments!


Come meet new people and have a blast! We can't wait to see you there!
