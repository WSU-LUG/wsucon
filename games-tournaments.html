WSUCon is host to many different types of games. WSUCon isn't really just a LAN party, it's a complete social gaming event. We have 3 different areas set up for all sorts of gaming and we've got tournaments planned that include computer, card, and console games! Participation in WSUCon is based on what you want to do. You can participate in tournaments, have a blast at just gaming by yourself or with friends. It's all up to you!
